Apply to make a compulsory purchase order (CPO)

Apply to make a compulsory purchase order in England and Wales. A CPO allows certain bodies to acquire land or rights without the owner's consent where it is necessary and in the public interest.

This service is for acquiring authorities preparing and submitting a CPO for confirmation.

Before you start

You will need to prepare and upload the documents required for a CPO application.

These may include:

  • the Compulsory Purchase Order
  • the Order Map
  • the Schedule of Interests
  • the Statement of Reasons
  • supporting documents for the scheme

It will take around 30 to 45 minutes to complete the application. You can save your progress and return later.
